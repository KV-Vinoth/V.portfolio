Notify App — UI/UX Case Study

Description :

    This application is service-based and aimed at assisting individuals seeking government jobs. It helps users determine their suitable career paths. Once a government job is posted, the app sends timely notifications tailored to the user’s preferences. It customizes notifications based on the individual's specific job preferences within the government sector.

    Additionally, the app aids users in situations where multiple government job postings occur simultaneously. Often, individuals tend to focus on only one or two job opportunities, leaving other vacancies unexplored. This app ensures that all available job vacancies are brought to the user's attention, preventing missed opportunities.
